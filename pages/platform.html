Direct integration with Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini APIs. When users ask for recommendations, these AI platforms call our BAIS system to find and book with your business.

  • GraphQL support
  • Webhook notifications
  • SDK libraries

Business System Connectors

Pre-built connectors to popular business systems - booking platforms, payment processors, inventory management. We connect to your existing tools so AI users can book directly.
