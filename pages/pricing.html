FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

What happens if I exceed my API limits?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limits. You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional API calls as needed.

Do you offer custom pricing?

Yes, for Enterprise customers with specific requirements, we offer custom pricing and features. Contact our sales team to discuss.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 14-day free trial for all plans. No credit card required to get started.

What integrations are included?

All plans include access to our library of 50+ pre-built integrations including Shopify, Amazon, Google, Salesforce, and more.

How is support provided?

Starter includes email support, Professional includes 24/7 priority support, and Enterprise includes a dedicated account manager.
