About Us

We're Building the Future of AI Commerce

BAIS is a new platform pioneering AI commerce. We're working with visionary businesses to build the future of how consumers discover and buy through AI.

Mission

Our Mission

To connect businesses with 34+ million daily AI users through Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini. We're building the platform that lets AI users discover and buy from your business without leaving their conversation.

Vision

Our Vision

Think Stripe for AI purchases - one integration that works across all major AI platforms. When someone asks Claude for a restaurant recommendation, they can book a table. When they ask ChatGPT for a hotel, they can make a reservation. That's the future we're building.

Our Story

Why We Started BAIS

We started BAIS in 2025 when we realized something obvious: 34+ million people use Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini daily, and they're already asking for business recommendations. But there was no way for them to actually buy from those businesses.


So we built the platform that connects AI users with businesses. When someone asks Claude "Find me a hotel in San Francisco," they can now book it directly. When they ask ChatGPT for restaurant recommendations, they can make a reservation. No leaving the AI conversation.


We're still early. We don't have thousands of customers or years of case studies. What we have is working technology and a massive market opportunity. The businesses that join us now will be the ones that dominate AI commerce.
