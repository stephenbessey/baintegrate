Our Story

Building the Autonomous Economy

Founded in 2024, BA Integrate emerged from a simple observation: as AI agents become increasingly sophisticated, businesses need standardized ways to interact with them. We recognized that without common protocols, the autonomous economy would remain fragmented and inefficient.


Our team of AI researchers, software engineers, and business strategists came together to create the Business-Agent Integration Standard (BAIS) - a comprehensive protocol that enables AI agents to seamlessly interact with hospitality, restaurant, and retail businesses.


Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of this transformation, helping businesses across industries prepare for a future where AI agents handle routine operations, freeing human talent to focus on innovation and growth.
